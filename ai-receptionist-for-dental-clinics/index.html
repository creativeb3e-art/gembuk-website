FAQs

Common questions from dental clinics.

Does this replace our receptionist?

No. It covers overflow and after-hours so your team can focus on in-clinic patients and priority admin.

What happens if a patient wants to book immediately?

The assistant captures preferred times and reason for visit, then sends a clear summary for fast confirmation. (Optional integrations can be discussed later.)

Can we control what it says?

Yes. We tailor the script to your clinic’s services, hours, policies (e.g., emergency handling), and preferred tone.

How quickly can we go live?

Typically within a few days once we have your clinic details and call routing confirmed.

What about accents and pronunciation?

We can tune the voice and phrasing to sound natural for NZ/AU callers and adjust pronunciation for clinic names and local terms.

What does the clinic need to provide?

Hours, services, booking preferences, emergency rules, and a destination email/number for summaries. We’ll guide you through a short checklist.
